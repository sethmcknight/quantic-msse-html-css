The Beach Blawg

I had wanted to be a lawyer ever since I was little. But for some reason, once I started blogging on the side after law school, my motivation to work my 9 to 5 sort of faded away. One day I finally made the decision that had been looming over me for years. I created this blog to share my experiences and provide a new perspective on drastic lifestyle changes. My mantra is to always enjoy every moment of existence. I hope you enjoy.

An Argument Against Sand -posted on January 23rd

I've been here for 3 days and I am already questioning my decision. My skin is literally drying up, the temperature is too hot, the air feels like fire, and everything smells like sand. This place has absolutely nothing that I need or want. The weather is miserable and there's absolutely no sun because it's always cloudy. All I want to do is go back home where my skin can breathe and my trousers have less sand. Hopefully these feelings pass soon, I think I might just need some coffee...
