Hart Cookie Recipe

My Aunt Frances would have these warm and cookies all the time, even if we surprised her with a visit!
Family members just recently compared their version of her famous cookie recipe only to find that each of us had a different variation!
We all concluded that getting accurate measurements from her is next to impossible. :)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup Crisco or butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Cream butter and add flour, salt, and baking powder.
  2. Beat eggs, sugar, and vanilla. Mix together.
  3. Shape cookie dough into hearts on cookie sheets then chill for 30 minutes.
  4. Bake at 300 degrees for about 20 minutes or until slightly brown. Let cool.
